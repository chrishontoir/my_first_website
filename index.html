About Me

Hello! I'm Chris, and I'm 25 years old. I'm from a small town called Porthcawl, which is located in South Wales.
I went to West Park Primary School and Porthcawl Comprehensive School where I studied A Levels in Computing, History & Music, before going to study Music at Cardiff University.

Interests

I play three musical instruments: piano, violin and clarinet, although I consider piano to be my main instrument. I enjoy writing music for piano and listening to film scores.
I also enjoy reading horror books, watching movies, and want to start learning another language.

Coding

I first started coding in Visual Basic for A Level Computing. I created a booking system for a cinema that allowed customers to select their seats, complete their booking and print their tickets.
Since then I self-taught myself HTML, CSS and a very small amount of JavaScript to create websites myself.
